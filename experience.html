Experience

Hello

With over 10 years experience within the Creative Industry as a Senior Designer/Creative Artworker, I’ve specialised in point of sale, packaging and digital assets. I have extensive experience in, in-store point of sale for major industry brands. I am a senior level creative who has worked in studios which deliver some of the industries most recognised names.

My various roles have equipped me with the skills necessary to work both agency side and client facing. I take conceptual briefs direct from the client, and take that brief right through to print ready artwork. I ensure that deadlines are met and budgets adhered to and all the while ensuring client satisfaction.

My work can be seen all over the high street as well as online, I have shown a few of the pieces I enjoyed working on the most, later on in my CV.
